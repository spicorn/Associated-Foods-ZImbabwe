About AFZ

Associated Foods Zimbabwe is a leading food manufacturer and distributor of quality Spreads, Canned Products, Preserves and Snack Foods in Zimbabwe. We strive to deliver customer needs and to add value to the Food industry through the provision of affordable, quality edibles manufactured through cost effective methods and procedures whilst ensuring that the interests of all stakeholders are honored.
